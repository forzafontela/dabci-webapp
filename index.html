DABCI DIPLOMATE PROGRAM

The Diplomate of the American Board of Chiropractic Internists (DABCI) seeks to maintain & promote the chiroproactic profession as primary healthcare. DABCI supports the advancement of the chiropractic profession, influences full scope practice within the profession and is involved in current and future health reform legislation.
